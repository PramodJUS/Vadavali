Vadavali by Sri Jayatirtha - A Treatise on Dvaita Vedanta

वादावली

Vadavali (वादावली) is a seminal philosophical text composed by Sri Jayatirtha (also known as Teeka Acharya), presenting the core principles of Dvaita (Dualistic) Vedanta philosophy.

द्वैत वेदान्त सिद्धान्ताः

  • पञ्चभेद: Five-fold eternal difference
  • स्वतन्त्र-परतन्त्र: Independent Brahman, dependent jīva
  • विष्णु-सर्वोत्तमता: Supremacy of Vishnu
  • तत्त्ववाद: Realism - differences are real
